1. Al Nahyan Family

  • Estimated Wealth: $305 billion

  • Company: Abu Dhabi Royal Family


  • The Al Nahyan family, also referred to as the "House of Nahyan," is the richest family in the world. The leader of the family, Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, is the ruler of Abu Dhabi and the President of the United Arab Emirates (UAE). The economy of the UAE (and the finances of its rulers and royal families) has been transformed by the vast oil reserves in the country. Abu Dhabi is one of the richest emirates, and especially oil-rich. Sheikh Mohamed became the ruler of Abu Dhabi and the President of the UAE in 2022. Before that, his brother, Sheikh Khalifa, was the country's leader from 2004 to 2022. And before that, his father, Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan, was the President of the country, from its founding in 1971 until 2004, when his son succeeded him
